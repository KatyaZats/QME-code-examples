Here are code examples from the *Quantum Mpemba Effect with Josephson Junctions* project.

The repository contains three notebooks corresponding to different orbital numbers and physical parameters:

- **Greens functions, QME, l=1.ipynb** – small quantum dot length, without spin–orbit interaction (SOI) and Zeeman field (ZF);
- **Greens functions, QME, l=2.ipynb** – intermediate quantum dot length, without SOI and ZF;
- **Greens functions, QME, SOI and ZF.ipynb)** – intermediate quantum dot length including spin–orbit interaction and Zeeman field.

In each of the cases, we compute: the energies of the corresponding Andreev bound states (ABSs), transition rates ABSs -> ABSs and ABSs -> continuum states, steady state occupation probabilities (many-body states) - from the Lindblad master equation, and relaxation times for the superconducting phase quench - from the Lindblad master equation.
